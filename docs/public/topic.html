Jujutsu Kaisen is a Anime-Manga series written by Gege Akutami , the main plot is about the fight between Sorcerers and Curses,curses are bad creatures created because of the hated and evil feelings of all humans on earth ,and in the other hand the sorcerers are people capable of controlling cursed energy and develop certain cursed techniques as Domain Expansions.
Domain Expansions are only capable of develop by extreme talented jujutsu sorceres and when used in a fight they almost guaranteed you victory, as you can manipulate that space as you want.
My favourite character of the series is called Toji Fushiguro , he is a human , part of the Zenin Clan , Blesed by the gods with a curse when he was born that eliminates all cursed energy of his body in exchange of superhuman strenght and instincts,he is father of Megumi Fushiguro ,one of the main side characters,and it inspires me to keep going to the gym >:).
